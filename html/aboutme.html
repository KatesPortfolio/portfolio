ABOUT ME

Hi, I´m Katarina

This site is a collection of my journey so far. With a degree in Media Technical Software Development and Design makes my passion for turning creative designs into functional code. I love the process of building things from scratch and have a keen eye for detail (yes, I’m the one who needs every pixel to be in its right place!) When I’m not developing, I pour my creativity into my art. I even run a small side business where I sell my work—it’s my favorite way to spend my weekends and a big part of who I am.

Take a look around to get to know both the developer and the artist!
